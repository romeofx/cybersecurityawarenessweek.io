About Exelsor Projects Ltd.

Committed to promoting digital safety and cybersecurity awareness across Nigeria.

Our Work and Impact

Exelsor Projects Limited provides Information and Communication Technology solutions that support organizations seeking to modernize operations and improve efficiency. The company works with institutions across government, education, business, healthcare, and other sectors to integrate practical technology solutions into everyday processes.

Our team supports organizations through technology advisory services, digital transformation initiatives, application development, and IT infrastructure improvement. These solutions help institutions strengthen internal systems, improve productivity, and respond effectively to modern digital demands.

Exelsor Projects Limited organizes workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to educate individuals and businesses on best practices for protecting their online presence.

The organization partners with schools, corporate institutions, and public sector agencies to promote digital literacy and strengthen cybersecurity awareness across communities.

Who We Are

Exelsor Projects Limited operates as an Information and Communication Technology service provider focused on helping organizations adopt modern technology tools that improve efficiency and support strategic growth.

Founded in February 2018 and registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in June 2019, the company maintains its headquarters in Abakaliki, Nigeria and an international branch in Swindon, United Kingdom.

Exelsor delivers services across government, security, education, healthcare, business, hospitality, and social sectors while supporting both institutions and individuals seeking practical technology solutions.

What We Do

Exelsor applies technology solutions that align business goals with digital systems. The organization helps institutions modernize their operations through automation, application development, and infrastructure optimization.

Services include business process automation, software development, IT advisory, desktop virtualization, and digital transformation support which help organizations reduce operational cost and improve system management.

Vision

To become a dependable technology partner recognized for delivering quality solutions, strong value, and timely services that support corporate strategy and simplify business challenges through modern technology.

Mission

To provide world class IT products, professional training, and reliable technology services that enable organizations and individuals achieve their goals through modern digital innovation.

Our Commitment

Exelsor Projects Limited continues to support organizations seeking to embrace modern digital tools by combining professional expertise, practical training, and emerging technology to deliver efficient and sustainable solutions.

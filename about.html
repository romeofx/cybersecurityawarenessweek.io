About Exelsor Projects Ltd.

Committed to promoting digital safety and cybersecurity awareness across Nigeria.

Our Mission

Exelsor Projects Limited organizes workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to educate individuals and businesses on best practices for protecting their online presence.

We empower citizens with knowledge and tools to stay safe in the digital world. Our programs are designed to make cybersecurity accessible and practical for everyone, from students to business owners.

Exelsor Projects Ltd. also collaborates with schools, organizations, and government bodies to strengthen digital resilience across communities.

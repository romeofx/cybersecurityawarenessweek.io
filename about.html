About Exelsor Projects Ltd.

Committed to promoting digital safety and cybersecurity awareness across Nigeria.

Our Work and Impact

Exelsor Projects Limited provides Information and Communication Technology solutions that support organizations seeking to modernize operations and improve efficiency. The company works with institutions across government, education, business, healthcare, and other sectors to integrate practical technology solutions into everyday processes.

Our team supports organizations through technology advisory services, digital transformation initiatives, application development, and IT infrastructure improvement. These solutions help institutions strengthen internal systems, improve productivity, and respond effectively to modern digital demands.

Exelsor Projects Limited organizes workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to educate individuals and businesses on best practices for protecting their online presence.

The organization also partners with schools, corporate institutions, and public sector agencies to promote digital literacy, strengthen cybersecurity awareness, and support the responsible use of technology across communities.

Who We Are

Exelsor Projects Limited operates as an Information and Communication Technology service provider. The company focuses on helping organizations adopt modern technology tools which improve operational efficiency and support strategic growth.

Founded in February 2018 and registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in June 2019, Exelsor maintains its headquarters in Abakaliki, Nigeria. The company also operates an international branch in Swindon, United Kingdom with supporting staff across both locations.

Exelsor delivers services across several sectors including government, security, education, healthcare, business, hospitality, sports, and social organizations. The firm supports both individuals and institutions seeking practical technology solutions.

What We Do

Exelsor applies technology solutions which align business goals with digital systems. The organization helps institutions modernize their operations through automation, application development, and infrastructure optimization.

Services include business process automation, software application development, IT advisory, desktop virtualization, and digital transformation support. These services help organizations reduce operational cost, improve system management, and expand access to new digital markets.

Vision

To become a recognized and dependable technology partner known for delivering quality solutions, strong value, and timely services which support corporate strategy and simplify business challenges through the effective use of technology.

Mission

To provide world-class IT products, professional training, and reliable technology services that enable organizations and individuals to achieve their goals while benefiting from modern digital innovation.

Our Commitment

Exelsor Projects Limited continues to support organizations seeking to embrace modern digital tools. The company combines professional expertise, practical training, and emerging technology to deliver efficient and sustainable solutions for clients.
